ABOUT THE BAND

Performing a wide repertoire of iconic hits and covers, This is Back to Black will captivate you with their infectious groove as they pay tribute to Amy Winehouse. Based

Based on the beautiful island of Mallorca, the band consists of experienced musicians from across the globe, including the powerful voice of Sofia Di Vuono, born in England and a graduate of The Brit School of Performing Arts in London where Amy also was a student. Accompanying her are fantastic musicians who have played on stages around the world.
